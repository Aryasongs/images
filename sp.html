Biography

"ਸ਼ਰਨ ਪੂਨੀ, ਬੰਗਾ (ਪੰਜਾਬ) ਦਾ ਇੱਕ ਉਭਰਦਾ ਪੰਜਾਬੀ ਕਲਾਕਾਰ ਹੈ, ਜੋ ਆਪਣੀ ਭਾਵੁਕ ਆਵਾਜ਼ ਅਤੇ ਦਿਲ ਨੂੰ ਛੂਹਣ ਵਾਲੇ ਸੰਗੀਤ ਲਈ ਜਾਣਿਆ ਜਾਂਦਾ ਹੈ।"

Sharan Pooni, born as Gursharan Singh, is an emerging Punjabi music artist from Banga, Punjab, India. He completed his education at a government school, where his passion for music began at an early age.

Driven by deep emotions and real-life experiences, Sharan Pooni is not only a singer but also a composer and music producer, known for creating soulful and emotionally rich Punjabi music. His artistic approach reflects honesty, pain, and storytelling that connects directly with listeners.

"His debut song 'Paper' earned widespread appreciation for its emotional depth and originality."

He made his official music debut with the song “Paper,” a heartfelt Punjabi sad track released in collaboration with renowned Punjabi singer Jass Bajwa, who lent his powerful voice to the song. The lyrics were written by Sharan Pooni’s friend, while the composition and production were handled by Sharan himself.

“Paper” marked a strong and promising entry into the Punjabi music industry. With a clear vision and dedication to his craft, Sharan Pooni continues to work toward establishing a unique identity in Punjabi music.

As a young artist with strong creative skills and authentic storytelling, Sharan Pooni represents the new generation of Punjabi music, and his journey has only just begun.
