Origins & Evolution

Born as Gursharan Singh in the vibrant soils of Banga, Punjab, Sharan Pooni's childhood was framed by the rhythmic pulse of rural India and the structured silence of a government school.

By Chapter II of his journey, Sharan transcended traditional performance to become a meticulous self-taught Producer and Composer. Every release under SharanRecords is a carefully crafted piece of emotional architecture.

"ਮੇਰਾ ਸੰਗੀਤ ਰੂਹ ਦੀ ਆਵਾਜ਼ ਹੈ, ਜੋ ਹਰ ਦੁੱਖ ਤੇ ਸੁੱਖ ਨੂੰ ਸ਼ਬਦਾਂ ਵਿੱਚ ਪਰੋਂਦਾ ਹੈ।"

The breakthrough single "Paper" with Jass Bajwa marked Sharan's definitive entry into the industry, proving that soulful storytelling still holds the ultimate power in modern Punjabi music.
