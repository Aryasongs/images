Genesis

Roots of a Visionary

Sharan Pooni entered the world as Gursharan Singh in the heart of Banga, Punjab. His formative years were spent amidst the rustic charm of rural landscapes and the disciplined walls of a government school.

It was here that an intuitive resonance with sound began to evolve into a serious passion. Sharan's journey is a rare example of raw talent meeting relentless curiosity.

"ਮੇਰਾ ਸੰਗੀਤ ਰੂਹ ਦੀ ਆਵਾਜ਼ ਹੈ, ਜੋ ਹਰ ਦੁੱਖ ਤੇ ਸੁੱਖ ਨੂੰ ਸ਼ਬਦਾਂ ਵਿੱਚ ਪਰੋਂਦਾ ਹੈ।"

Ascension

Architect of Sound

Beyond his vocal prowess, Sharan established himself as a meticulous Music Producer and Composer. His debut landmark "Paper" featuring the legendary Jass Bajwa, wasn't just a release—it was a declaration of his creative identity.

By managing composition and production under his banner SharanRecords, he ensures every note is infused with authentic storytelling and emotional depth.

Manifesto

"I don't create trends. I capture the truth in human emotion."

— Sharan Pooni
