The Roots

Born on February 02, 2006, in Banga, Punjab, Gursharan Singh's journey began with a latent passion for music first stirring within school walls.

"ਸੰਗੀਤ ਸਿਰਫ ਆਵਾਜ਼ ਨਹੀਂ, ਇਹ ਮੇਰੀਆਂ ਭਾਵਨਾਵਾਂ ਦਾ ਪ੍ਰਗਟਾਵਾ ਹੈ।"

Landmark Breakthrough

Sharan's official entry into the industry featured the landmark track "Paper" with Jass Bajwa, produced and composed by Sharan himself under SharanRecords.
