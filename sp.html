Biography

"ਸ਼ਰਨ ਪੂਨੀ, ਬੰਗਾ (ਪੰਜਾਬ) ਦਾ ਇੱਕ ਉਭਰਦਾ ਪੰਜਾਬੀ ਕਲਾਕਾਰ ਹੈ, ਜੋ ਆਪਣੀ ਭਾਵੁਕ ਆਵਾਜ਼ ਲਈ ਜਾਣਿਆ ਜਾਂਦਾ ਹੈ।"

Sharan Pooni, born Gursharan Singh, is an emerging Punjabi music artist from Banga. Known for his debut "Paper" featuring Jass Bajwa, Sharan represents the new wave of soulful Punjabi music.

As a singer, lyricist, and producer, he maintains creative control over his sound, blending real-life stories with emotionally rich melodies.
