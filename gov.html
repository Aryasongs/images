District SBS Nagar

The district of Shaheed Bhagat Singh Nagar (formerly Nawanshahr) is a hub of development in the Doaba region. Known for its strong NRI connections and agricultural productivity, the administration is committed to making it a prime destination for sustainable investment and high quality of life.
